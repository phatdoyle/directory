Artist behind Punkism - art inspired by CryptoPunks, reimagined through the lenses of Warhol, Picasso, Rembrandt, film stills, memes, and comic art. Also creates physical oil paintings exploring CryptoPunk traits as fine art. Collaborator with Tom Lehman on on-chain projects. Featured in the Art of Punk exhibition alongside Beeple, Snowfro, and Tom Sachs.

Museum Punks documents the journey of CryptoPunks into the permanent collections of major art institutions worldwide, exploring the intersection of digital art, punk culture, and institutional recognition.

## The Shape of Recognition

CryptoPunks have found their way into some of the world's most prestigious museums:

- **MoMA** - Museum of Modern Art, New York
- **Centre Pompidou** - Paris
- **LACMA** - Los Angeles County Museum of Art
- **ICA Miami** - Institute of Contemporary Art

## The Punks Legacy Project

Launched in November 2022 with its inaugural donation to ICA Miami, this initiative oversees the donation and installation of CryptoPunks in museums' permanent collections, supplemented by education in web3, crypto security, and NFT best practices.
